Features

Based on Spotify's music collection*

Never get tired of listening to the same stuff over and over again, with a collection of millions of songs. Drag and drop your track directly from the Spotify app, or use our search tool.

*Requires a Premium Spotify account

Let the DJ know you love (or hate) what's playing anonymously

There are some songs that work well in a public space, there are others that are definitely for headphones only. Let the DJ know what works and what doesn't.

Skip the current song

If too many people down-vote the same song, it gets scratched out and skipped. We call it the Chas and Dave Buster.

Reward people's interactions

We baked in a badge system that adds a level of exploration to the radio.

Reward good interactions, punish bad interactions and hide surprises in the system.

Real-time statistics of what's happening

See who's the most liked DJ, what songs ranked best this week and how many votes your songs had.

How it works

A user queues a song on the server through their browser, and the Raspberry PI requests the next track in the queue and plays it through your speakers. Push notifications keep all clients in sync with what is going on.

How to get it

The various parts of the application have now been released in an beta state.

It's been running almost flawlessly for us at Poke for the last few months, however there are a lot of moving parts and we can't guarantee setup will be completely issue free for someone new to this.

This is aimed at developers that have had some experience of Django and Node.js as you may need to solve problems yourself as you encounter them.

FAQs

Why can I only use Spotify? I know Mopidy supports alternative backend suppliers such as SoundCloud...

We made a conscious choice to keep Spotify as our only music source - other options weren't right for us - for example SoundCloud is great, but generally has much longer audio assets which felt like it was against the collaborative nature of the player. If you want to add other sources feel free!

I'm using a Raspberry PI and the audio sounds terrible - what's wrong?

Make sure you follow the instructions on how to install Mopidy here. There are some additional tips on how to solve common audio issues with the PI.

Why can't I login with a username and password?

We chose to use Google as our authentication sign in handler as a google account is a convenient way to restrict access on a per domain basis. Feel free to implement a Django based user management system - it will work just as well.

Please can you add feature x or y?

We may well do, but we have our own internal roadmap based on the particular needs of Poke - they are unlikely to be the same for everyone, so if you want a feature added then have a go yourself!
